NETWORK TROUBLESHOOTING

Network troubleshooting is the process of identifying, diagnosing, and resolving problems in a computer network. These issues can range from slow internet connections and dropped connections to hardware malfunctions and security breaches. Effective network troubleshooting requires a systematic approach, including analyzing network logs, testing network equipment, and identifying potential causes of problems. By troubleshooting network issues, organizations can ensure optimal network performance, minimize downtime, and enhance overall network security.
